# IMP-01: important 优先级测试
# 需求: lineProps://important 的规则应优先于普通规则

# IMP-01 普通规则 vs important 规则 (后定义的 important 优先)
imp-test.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Winner:normal-first`
imp-test.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Winner:important` lineProps://important

# IMP-02 important vs important (先定义的 important 优先，因为 priority 相同时按定义顺序)
imp-both.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Both:first-important` lineProps://important
imp-both.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Both:second-important` lineProps://important

# IMP-03 通配符 important vs 精确普通
*.imp-wildcard.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Wildcard:important-wildcard` lineProps://important
test.imp-wildcard.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Wildcard:exact-normal`

# IMP-04 较低优先级的 important vs 较高优先级的普通
*.*.imp-deep.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Deep:deep-important` lineProps://important
test.imp-deep.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Deep:shallow-normal`

# IMP-05 important 不影响协议的多匹配特性
imp-multi.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resHeaders://`X-Multi:first`
imp-multi.local http://127.0.0.1:3000 resAppend://`<!--appended-->` lineProps://important

# 测试用例:
# 1. GET http://imp-test.local/ 应返回 X-Winner: important
# 2. GET http://imp-both.local/ 应返回 X-Both: first-important
# 3. GET http://test.imp-wildcard.local/ 应返回 X-Wildcard: important-wildcard
# 4. GET http://a.test.imp-deep.local/ 应返回 X-Deep: deep-important
